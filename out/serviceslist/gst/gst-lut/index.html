What is GST LUT (Letter of Undertaking)?

GST LUT (Letter of Undertaking) is a declaration filed by exporters under the Goods and Services Tax Act that allows them to export goods or services without paying Integrated GST (IGST). Instead of paying IGST and later claiming a refund, exporters can submit LUT and directly export without tax payment.

Who Can File LUT?

  • Registered GST taxpayers engaged in export of goods.
  • Service exporters.
  • Businesses supplying to SEZ units.

Benefits of GST LUT Filing

  • No need to block working capital by paying IGST.
  • Avoid refund delays.
  • Faster export processing.
  • Improved cash flow management.

GST LUT Filing Process

  1. Login to GST portal.
  2. Navigate to LUT filing section.
  3. Select financial year.
  4. Submit declaration.
  5. Receive ARN acknowledgment.

Documents Required for LUT Filing

  • GST login credentials
  • Authorized signatory details
  • Digital Signature (if applicable)
