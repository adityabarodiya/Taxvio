What is GST Amendment?

GST amendment refers to updating or modifying details in your existing GST registration. Businesses must update changes in business name, address, contact information, bank details, partners/directors or authorized signatory within prescribed timelines to remain compliant.

Types of GST Amendments

1. Core Fields Amendment

Core fields require approval from GST officer and include:

  • Business name change
  • Principal place of business
  • Addition/removal of partners or directors

2. Non-Core Fields Amendment

Non-core amendments are auto-approved and include:

  • Bank account details
  • Contact details (Email & Mobile)
  • Authorized signatory details

GST Amendment Process

  1. Login to GST portal.
  2. Select amendment option under services.
  3. Update required fields.
  4. Upload supporting documents.
  5. Submit using DSC/EVC verification.

Documents Required for GST Amendment

  • GST login credentials
  • Proof of address (if address changed)
  • Partnership deed / Board resolution
  • Bank statement or cancelled cheque
