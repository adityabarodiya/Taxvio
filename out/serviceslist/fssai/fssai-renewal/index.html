What is FSSAI License Renewal?

FSSAI License Renewal is the process of extending the validity of an existing food license. FSSAI licenses are generally valid for 1 to 5 years depending on the chosen registration period. Renewal must be applied for at least 30 days before expiry.

Failure to renew on time can attract a late fee of ₹100 per day and may lead to cancellation of the license.
