Understanding FSSAI Verification

What Is FSSAI License Search and How Does It Work?

FSSAI License Search is a free online verification service provided by the Food Safety and Standards Authority of India. It allows any individual, business, or regulatory body to verify the authenticity and current status of a food business operator's FSSAI registration or license using the unique 14-digit FSSAI license number printed on their certificate and food packaging.

The FSSAI license number is structured to carry meaningful information — the first digit indicates the state code, the next digits identify the license category and issuing office, and the remaining digits are the unique business identifier. By searching this number on the official Food Licensing and Registration System (FLRS) portal, anyone can instantly verify whether a food business is legally authorized to operate.

This verification tool is critical across multiple use cases — consumers verifying food brands before purchase, e-commerce platforms like Amazon and Flipkart onboarding new food sellers, institutional buyers checking supplier credentials, and food aggregators like Zomato and Swiggy auditing their restaurant partners.

At Taxvio, we go beyond just guiding you through the verification process. If the search reveals any compliance issue — expired license, wrong business details, suspended status, or incorrect product category — our FSSAI specialists step in immediately to identify the problem and resolve it through the correct regulatory channel.

Step-by-Step Guide

How to Check FSSAI License Number Online — Step by Step

FSSAI license verification is a simple process on the official government portal. Here is exactly how to do it — and what to look for in the results.

1
Step 01

Visit the Official FSSAI Verification Portal

Go to the official Food Licensing and Registration System (FLRS) portal at foscos.fssai.gov.in or the dedicated license search page at fssai.gov.in. These are the only authorised government portals for FSSAI verification — do not rely on third-party websites.

2
Step 02

Navigate to the License Search Section

On the portal homepage, look for the 'License/Registration Search' or 'Verify License' option. This is available without login — no account creation is needed to perform a basic verification check.

3
Step 03

Enter the 14-Digit FSSAI License Number

Type the complete 14-digit FSSAI license or registration number exactly as printed on the food business certificate or food product packaging. Even a single incorrect digit will return no results or incorrect information.

4
Step 04

Complete the Captcha Verification

Enter the captcha code displayed on screen to proceed. This is a standard security measure on the government portal to prevent automated bulk queries.

5
Step 05

Review the License Details and Status

The portal displays the complete license record including the business name, license type, registered address, product categories, validity period, and current status. Review all fields carefully for accuracy and active status.

6
Step 06

Act on Any Issues Found

If the license shows expired, suspended, cancelled, or contains incorrect details, contact Taxvio immediately. Our team will identify the correct remediation path — whether that is a renewal, modification, notice reply, or fresh registration.

What the Search Shows

What Information Is Displayed in FSSAI Search Results?

When you search a valid FSSAI license number, the portal displays a comprehensive record of the food business. Here is what each field means and why it matters.

👤

Business Operator Name

The registered name of the Food Business Operator (FBO) — individual, partnership, or company.

✓ What to check: Verify this matches the name on packaging or invoices.

🔢

14-Digit License Number

The unique identifier assigned by FSSAI. The first digit is the state code; subsequent digits identify the office and business.

✓ What to check: Confirms the number is genuine and matches official records.

📋

License Type

Indicates whether the business holds a Basic Registration, State License, or Central License.

✓ What to check: Verify the license type matches the scale of operations described.

📅

Validity Period

The start and end dates of the current license registration period (1 to 5 years from issuance).

✓ What to check: Confirm the license is currently valid and has not lapsed.

📍

Registered Business Address

The official address of the food business premises as registered with FSSAI.

✓ What to check: Match with actual operating address — discrepancies need modification.

🟢

Current Status

Shows the real-time status: Active, Expired, Suspended, or Cancelled.

✓ What to check: Only 'Active' status confirms legal authorization to operate.

Status Definitions

Understanding FSSAI License Status Results

The status field is the most critical piece of information in the FSSAI search result. Here is what each status means and what action is required.

✅ Active

The food business holds a valid, currently active FSSAI license within its validity period. The business is legally authorized to operate, manufacture, distribute, or sell food products.

→ Required Action: No immediate action required. Monitor expiry date and renew 30–60 days before it lapses.

⏰ Expired

The food license validity period has lapsed. The food business is currently operating without a valid FSSAI license, which is illegal under the FSS Act, 2006. A ₹100/day penalty is accumulating.

→ Required Action: Apply for FSSAI renewal immediately. Taxvio can process your renewal application and manage penalty calculation.

⛔ Suspended

The food safety authority has temporarily suspended the license due to non-compliance. The business must cease all food operations immediately. Continued operation is a criminal offence.

→ Required Action: Engage a compliance professional urgently. Taxvio will prepare a reinstatement application, compliance documentation, and coordinate with the food authority for license restoration.

❌ Cancelled

The food license has been permanently cancelled by the authority following serious violations or failure to comply with suspension orders. The business cannot legally operate under this license.

→ Required Action: Legal consultation is required. In some cases, cancellation can be appealed before the Food Safety Appellate Tribunal. Alternatively, a fresh FSSAI registration may be pursued.

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions — FSSAI License Search

How can I verify an FSSAI license number online?+
Visit the official FSSAI portal at foscos.fssai.gov.in, navigate to the license search section, enter the complete 14-digit FSSAI license number, complete the captcha, and view the license record. The result shows the business name, license type, validity period, and current status.
What does a 14-digit FSSAI number mean?+
The 14-digit FSSAI license number is a structured unique identifier. The first two digits indicate the state code, the next two identify the license category and year, and the remaining digits form the unique business registration number. This structure means every FSSAI number carries traceable information about the issuing state and business.
What if the FSSAI license search shows 'No Record Found'?+
A 'No Record Found' result typically means the license number entered is incorrect, the business has not obtained FSSAI registration, or the registration was recently issued and not yet updated on the portal. Double-check the number. If it is correct and no record exists, the business may be operating illegally. Contact Taxvio for a compliance assessment.
Is FSSAI license verification mandatory for consumers?+
Verification is not legally mandatory for consumers, but it is strongly recommended — especially for packaged food, online food orders, or catering engagements. Checking the FSSAI number printed on packaging against the portal confirms the food business is legally registered and currently authorized to sell food.
Can I verify FSSAI license for free?+
Yes. The FSSAI license search tool on the official FLRS portal is completely free to use. No registration, login, or payment is required for a basic license number verification.
My FSSAI search shows expired — what should I do?+
Stop operating immediately and apply for renewal as quickly as possible. A ₹100/day penalty is accumulating from the expiry date. Taxvio can process your renewal application urgently, calculate your total penalty liability, and file the application through the official portal to restore your active status.
How do I update incorrect details on my FSSAI license?+
If the search reveals incorrect business name, address, or product categories, you need to apply for an FSSAI License Modification through the FLRS portal. Taxvio handles the complete modification process, ensuring the updated details are accurately reflected on your FSSAI certificate.
