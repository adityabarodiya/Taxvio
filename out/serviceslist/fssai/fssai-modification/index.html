FSSAI License Modification Online – Update Food License Details Easily

FSSAI License Modification is required whenever there is a change in business details such as address, business name, product category, ownership structure, contact details, or production capacity. Food Business Operators (FBOs) must update their license to remain compliant with the Food Safety and Standards Act, 2006.

Taxvio provides complete FSSAI license modification services including documentation, online filing, compliance review and government coordination. We assist food businesses in Khatauli, Muzaffarnagar, Noida, Delhi, Mumbai and across India.

What is FSSAI License Modification?

FSSAI Modification refers to updating or amending existing license details registered with FSSAI authorities. Any material change in business operations must be reported to ensure the license remains valid.

Operating with incorrect or outdated license details may attract penalties or suspension.

When is FSSAI Modification Required?

  • Change in Business Address
  • Change in Business Name
  • Addition or Removal of Food Products
  • Change in Contact Details
  • Increase in Production Capacity
  • Change in Ownership (Proprietor/Partner/Director)
  • Conversion from Basic to State or Central License

Types of FSSAI Modification

1. Minor Modification

Includes changes such as phone number, email address, minor corrections, or non-structural updates.

2. Major Modification

Includes change in business premises, addition of new product categories, structural changes, or change in ownership. Major modifications may require additional approval and inspection.

Documents Required for FSSAI License Modification

  • Existing FSSAI License Copy
  • Updated Business Address Proof (if applicable)
  • Revised Product List (if applicable)
  • Partnership Deed / MOA / AOA (if ownership changes)
  • Board Resolution (for companies)
  • Food Safety Management Plan
