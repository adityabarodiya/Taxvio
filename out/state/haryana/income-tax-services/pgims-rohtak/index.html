Income Tax FAQs for PGIMS Rohtak Taxpayers

What is the ITR filing deadline for PGIMS Rohtak taxpayers?

For individuals (salaried, proprietors) in PGIMS Rohtak, the ITR filing deadline is 31st July of the assessment year. For businesses requiring audit, the deadline is 31st October. Late filing attracts a penalty of up to ₹5,000 under Section 234F.

Do I need to file ITR in PGIMS Rohtak if my income is below the tax limit?

Filing is not mandatory if income is below the basic exemption limit (₹2.5 lakh for individuals below 60, ₹3 lakh for senior citizens). However, filing is advisable to claim TDS refunds, apply for loans, or obtain visas — and is mandatory if total TDS deducted exceeds ₹25,000/50,000 in certain cases.

How long does tax audit take for PGIMS Rohtak businesses?

Tax audit under Section 44AB for PGIMS Rohtak businesses typically takes 2–4 weeks, depending on the completeness of books of accounts and availability of vouchers. The audit report (Form 3CA/3CB-3CD) must be filed by 30th September, and ITR by 31st October for audited cases.
